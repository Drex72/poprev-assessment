Perspective 1 (Customer Perspective)
    Come onto the Application
    Create an account
    Log into your account
    See all Projects available for contribution
    Select a project to contribute
    Invest into the project and you receive tokens worth the amount you invested
    Check your poprev wallet to see your token information

Perspective 2 (Artist Perspective)
    Come onto the Application
    Create an account
    Log into your account
    Create a proposal for your project
    Wait for your proposal to be approved or rejected

Perspective 3 (Admin perspective)
    Come onto the Application
    Create an account
    Log into your account
    See all Proposals 
    Approve or Reject a proposal
    If a proposal is Approved, it gets converted into a project
